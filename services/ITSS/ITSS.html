ITSS is an IT-focused business and technical entity of CypheCrescent Limited that manages IT needs of both CypherCrescent group of companies and other non-domain client's requirements.   • Cloud Computing Services
  • Customised Application Development
  • Custom ERP Solutions (DIAP, etc.)
  • Data Protection and Security
  • Enterprise Information Management (SharePoint)
  • IT Consulting and Research
  • Managed IT Services
  • Network and Connectivity
  • Software Development & DevOps Services
  • Training
  • Virtualisation and Data Center Infrastructure
