Why CypherCrescent?

CypherCrescent is known for its technical excellence, leading-edge research, innovative solutions, and outstanding client service. We are collective people from different parts of the world that come together to collaborate in a bid to make a difference in our industry. We create a platform for people to push boundaries of Research &Development and create outstanding innovations.

Our interests are both in Technical and Non-Technical roles for individuals who are curious, knowledge and innovation driven, skilled and culture fit to join our team. Working in CypherCrescent means feeling empowered to break the frontiers of knowledge. We recognize hard work and we’re passionate about your growth and development while working with us.

Have an idea? Pitch it – “Innovative minds thrive”.

Our Ideal technical candidate is a professional with a skillful blend of mathematics, petroleum engineering, software development competencies and excellent communication skills. We are keenly interested in young graduates that think out of the box with ability to translate physical problems to mathematical models with the aim of proffering solutions to a wide spectrum of engineering and physical problems.
